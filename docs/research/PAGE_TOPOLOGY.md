# Page Topology

## Homepage (https://www.jiyuancap.com/)
- **Navbar**: Sticky top, logo on left, links on right. Background transitions from transparent at scroll 0 to blurred black.
- **Hero Section**: Background video (wavy/abstract), central text "Jiyuan Capital".
- **About Us**: Section with 3D cube animation and descriptive text.
- **Portfolios**: Grid of portfolio company logos/cards.
- **Investment Philosophy**: Text-heavy section about their approach.
- **Industry Insights**: Carousel/Slider with podcasts/articles.
- **OMEGA Plan**: Stats-heavy section about their accelerator program.
- **Contact Us**: Form or contact details at the bottom.
- **Footer**: Standard links and copyright.

## Family (https://www.jiyuancap.com/family/)
- **Hero**: Page title "Family".
- **Industry Filter**: Tabbed interface to filter portfolio companies.
- **Portfolio Grid**: Dynamic grid of companies based on selected industry.

## OMEGA (https://www.jiyuancap.com/omega/)
- **Hero**: OMEGA branding.
- **Program Stats**: Large numbers showing reach/impact.
- **Alumni**: Grid of companies that participated.
- **Batch Info**: Details about upcoming batches.

## Global Layout
- Scroll container: standard window scroll.
- Theme: Dark theme only.
- Animations: Reveal on scroll (fade-in, slide-up).
